About AF and D

African Foods and Deliveries brings buyers closer to their local food sellers with the aim of upholding outstanding purchasing standards. We believe that two heads are better than one, so are multiple heads better than a few. With the says of both the many buyers and sellers, a much greater standards can be achieved. This we try to acquire through our merchandise favorable programs such as our digital exhibitions, customer taskforce and advocacy, satisfaction level reviews, money-back guarantee, and lots more. Regardless of your class, AF & D helps you leverage on the strengths and opportunities of the local food market, thereby giving you more financial purchasing power to tend to the numerous other needs

Our Vision

We see AF and D optimally organising the overall production and acquisition of local foods in any locality with extreme standards, thereby making sure that all buyers get the best possible satisfaction they desire, and also making sure that sellers and producers go extra miles to gladly and satisfactorily make quality produce available for purchase.
